Alignment

CCPI is organizationally aligned to the VHA Office of Healthcare Innovation and Learning (OHIL), which is a key program under the VHA Office of Discovery, Education and Affiliate Networks (DEAN).

CCPI aligns to OHIL strategic priorities:

Advancing care outside the hospital walls.

Fostering personalized health care solutions.

Building and empowering the health care workforce of the future.

Forging the path for systematic, value-based care.

Creating sustainable health care for the Nation's Veterans and generations to come.
