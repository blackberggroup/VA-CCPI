Pilot Approach

Our approach to pilot design and implementation emphasizes:

Focus on measurably improving the healthcare outcomes that matter to Veterans.

Promote quality through access, equity, efficiency, and effectiveness, ensuring that all Veterans receive the care they need.

Advance VA's organizational priorities and address pressing, system-level challenges that require innovative solutions.

Our Mission

The Center for Care and Payment Innovation enhances value for Veterans and VA by designing and testing new ways to deliver and pay for care that efficiently use resources and improve healthcare outcomes.

What does CCPI do?

CCPI partners with VA Health Care systems, VA Integrated Service Networks, VA program offices, and industry leaders in executing three core capabilities:

Strategy

Drive alignment of enterprise-level policy, financing and clinical operations

Pilots

Test financial and service delivery models, including use of statutory waiver authority

Analytics

Use advanced analytic methods to identify solutions that yield the greatest benefit to Veterans and VA

CCPI's Waiver Authority

The MISSION Act provides us with authority to request waivers from Congress. Waivers give us flexibility to test new ways to deliver and pay for care that cannot be implemented under existing statutes. Learn more about our Waiver Authority on our About Us page.
