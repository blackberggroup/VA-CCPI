Pilot Approach

Our approach to pilot design and implementation emphasizes:

Focus on measurably improving the healthcare outcomes that matter to Veterans.

Promote quality through access, equity, efficiency, and effectiveness, ensuring that all Veterans receive the care they need.

Advance VA's organizational priorities and address pressing, system-level challenges that require innovative solutions.

Our Mission

The Center for Care and Payment Innovation enhances value for Veterans and VA by designing and testing new ways to deliver and pay for care that efficiently use resources and improve healthcare outcomes.
